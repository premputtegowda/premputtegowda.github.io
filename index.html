Intro

Hi, Thank you for visiting my portfolio site. I recently started my journey to becoming a software engineer. I am a former product lead, business analyst, product manager, and a quality assurance analyst. By becoming a software engineer, I would have played all the roles that are critical for product development. However, that is not the reason I started my journey as a software engineer. As a product lead, project manager and analyst, I worked closely with engineers and product developers. I was fascinated by the magic they performed with few lines of code they wrote. I liked the idea of using computers and solving real-world problems. I took a few online courses and ended up spending time coding all night without realizing it. I liked the idea of a rapid feedback loop that programming gave me. But I was hesistant. The insecurities of jumping starting something new and losing financial stability stopped me from taking action. One day I thought, progress doesn't happen by being secure and stable. It's the insecurities and the problems that we encounter that lead us a brighter future. So I started with Lambda school as a full-time software engineering/full stack developer student. I hope to become the best software engineer there can be and solve challenging problems and build great products. Please visit the worksection to check out some of the projects I have worked on.
